## Overview
This Tableau dashboard analyzes global BMW vehicle sales performance from 2010 to 2024, focusing on revenue, pricing, sales volume, and regional trends across vehicle models and fuel types.

## Dataset
The dataset contains vehicle-level sales information, including:
- Vehicle model and model year
- Sales region
- Fuel type and transmission
- Engine size and mileage
- Vehicle price (USD)
- Sales volume
- Sales classification (High / Low)

## Key Metrics
- Total Sales Revenue
- Average Vehicle Price
- Top Revenue-Generating Region

## Analytical Views
- Sales revenue comparison across global regions
- Sales volume distribution by vehicle model
- Average sales volume by region and model (heatmap)
- Revenue contribution by fuel type
- Yearly sales trends from 2010 to 2024
- Sales trends segmented by vehicle color

## Tool
- Tableau Desktop
